## 一、GovLink开场 & 双方介绍（15:00—15:08，8min）

### 【GovLink开场词】

Good morning / Good afternoon, everyone. This is [GovLink主持人姓名] from GovLink. On behalf of GovLink, welcome to this Kunshan-Chemnitz online meeting.

各位好，我是GovLink的[主持人姓名]。欢迎参加今天的昆山—开姆尼茨线上对接会议。

Let me briefly explain the background of today's meeting.

请允许我简要说明今天会议的背景。

GovLink is a professional business travel service provider based in Shanghai. We have been commissioned by the Kunshan Foreign Affairs Office to facilitate communication and cooperation between Kunshan and Chemnitz.

GovLink是一家总部位于上海的专业商务旅行服务机构。我们受昆山市人民政府外事办公室委托，协助推进昆山与开姆尼茨之间的沟通与合作。

Over the past period, we have been in contact with our German colleagues — Ms. Manuela Zenk from the Economic Affairs Department and Mr. Oliver Kietzmann from the Mayor's Office. We greatly appreciate their positive and professional response.

在过去一段时间里，我们与德方同事——经济事务部的Manuela Zenk女士和市长办公室的Oliver Kietzmann先生——保持了联络。非常感谢两位积极而专业的回应。

Through these preliminary communications, both sides have expressed initial interest in exploring a partnership, and Kunshan plans to visit Chemnitz this October.

通过前期的沟通，双方都对探讨合作表达了初步意向，昆山方面计划今年10月出访开姆尼茨。

Today's meeting is the **first direct dialogue** between the Kunshan Foreign Affairs Office and the Chemnitz city government. Our role today is to serve as the bridge — to bring both sides together, facilitate an open exchange, and lay the groundwork for the October visit.

今天的会议是 **昆山市外办与开姆尼茨市政府的首次直接对话**。我们今天在此的角色就是搭建桥梁——让双方正式会面，坦诚交流，为十月出访打下基础。

### 【PPT演示环节——昆山方面主讲】

（昆山外办主讲人共享PPT，按以下结构分Part讲解）

#### Part A：昆山城市概况（约5min）

要点：
- 长三角核心区位，上海-苏州之间
- 百强县之首，经济实力
- 装备制造3000亿、电子信息8000亿、新能源汽车完整产业链
- 150+德资企业、德国工业园、AHK德国商会、双元制职业教育

#### Part B：出访两大核心目标（约5min）

**目标一：建立友好交流城市关系**
> We propose to establish an official friendly exchange city relationship between Kunshan and Chemnitz. This would serve as the foundational framework — facilitating government dialogue, cultural exchange, and institutional cooperation.

**目标二：推动经济方向务实合作**
> Beyond the city relationship, we aim to drive concrete economic cooperation. Our industrial strengths are highly complementary — Kunshan's manufacturing scale and market with Chemnitz's engineering excellence and technology.

> These two objectives are two sides of the same coin. The friendly city relationship provides trust and framework; economic cooperation delivers tangible outcomes.

#### Part C：三程方向及各自拜访目的（约5min）

| 方向 | 拜访对象 | 拜访目的 | 期望产出 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| ①政府/议会 | 市长办公室、国际关系处、议会 | 正式提出友城关系，建立合作框架 | MOU/意向书、定期联络机制 |
| ②先进企业 | 机械制造/汽车/新能源等领域领先企业 | 了解技术实力，对接合作需求 | 企业联系渠道、技术合作/供应链协同 |
| ③大学与科研 | TU Chemnitz、Fraunhofer IWU/ENAS | 产学研合作：联合研发、人才交流 | 合作意向沟通、重点实验室参观 |

**Direction 1 参考话术：**
> Our purpose in meeting with the government and parliament is to formalize our partnership at the official level. We see this as the foundation — once we have this framework, all other forms of cooperation can develop more sustainably.

**Direction 2 参考话术：**
> Our goal for enterprise visits is twofold: first, to see Chemnitz's industrial excellence firsthand; second, to identify concrete opportunities where our enterprises can work together — whether through technology partnership, supply chain collaboration, or market access.

**Direction 3 参考话术：**
> In the academic sphere, we are interested in connecting Kunshan's innovation ecosystem with TU Chemnitz and the Fraunhofer Institutes — exploring joint research, technology transfer, and talent exchange programs.

#### Part D：产业互补分析（约4min）

用单页对比表展示：

| 领域   | 昆山优势       | 开姆尼茨优势                   | 合作方向       |
| :--- | :--------- | :----------------------- | :--------- |
| 机械制造 | 装备制造3000亿  | NSH, Starrag, TISORA     | 技术合作、供应链协同 |
| 汽车工业 | 新能源汽车完整产业链 | 大众发动机+零部件集群              | 技术对接、零部件合作 |
| 新能源  | 政策支持、市场巨大  | HIC氢能创新中心                | 氢能联合研发     |
| 数字经济 | 电子信息8000亿  | Staffbase、工业软件           | 数字化合作      |
| 科研教育 | 创新生态活跃     | TU Chemnitz + Fraunhofer | 产学研、双元制教育  |

#### Part E：德企生态展示（约3min）

> Kunshan is one of the most German-friendly cities in China — 150+ German enterprises, German Industrial Park, AHK presence, German-style vocational training. This demonstrates that Kunshan understands and values German business culture. We believe this creates a strong foundation for connecting with Chemnitz's industrial ecosystem.
